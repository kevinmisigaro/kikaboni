About Us

Kikaboni Farm Agribusiness LTD founded in 2019 is a registered and licensed agro – allied company that has its offices based in Bunju.

It is a specialized company that involves youths in production and supply of horticultural products and allied services. We Cultivate and supply horticultural crops such as; tomatoes, butternuts, green vegetables and bell peppers, also we are involved in agriculture related transplants and seed preparations.

OUR STORY

As Tanzania move to use its space on global market by implementing the industrial revolution policy it has opened more opportunities in different sectors the major being Agriculture as a source of raw material producing sector. This speed of development has led to the growth of horticultural sector. Currently the horticultural sector annual growth rate of about 9% to 12% per annum. With such a growth rate horticulture sector is now transforming and engaging more advance technologies ranging from area of production to irrigations systems which insure production efficiency. For this reason KIKABONI FARMS AGRIBUSNESS LIMITED was formed so as to engage more youth in horticulture using advanced farming technologies to ensure sustainable production and supply high quality horticultural products to our markets and processing industries.
